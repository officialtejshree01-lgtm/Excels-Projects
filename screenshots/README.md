# 📊 Excel Dashboard Portfolio (Project Screenshots)

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository showcases **screenshots of Excel Dashboard projects** developed across multiple domains.  
These dashboards demonstrate the use of **data analysis, visualization, and business intelligence techniques** to solve real-world problems.

The projects focus on:
- Financial tracking and risk analysis  
- Customer experience improvement  
- Inventory and supply chain management  

---

## 🧾 Projects Included

### 🏦 1. Bank Customer Loan Tracker  
**Domain:** Banking & Financial Services  

**Overview:**  
An Excel dashboard designed to track customer loans, monitor repayment status, and identify overdue or defaulted accounts.

**Key Highlights:**
- Loan tracking and repayment monitoring  
- Overdue & default analysis  
- Loan type distribution  
- Financial risk insights  

---

### 😊 2. Customer Feedback Analysis  
**Domain:** Customer Experience / Service Quality  

**Overview:**  
An interactive dashboard to analyze customer feedback, measure satisfaction, and identify service improvement areas.

**Key Highlights:**
- Customer rating analysis  
- Feedback category insights  
- Resolved vs pending tracking  
- Trend analysis of feedback  

---

### 📦 3. Retail Store Inventory Management  
**Domain:** Retail / Inventory & Supply Chain  

**Overview:**  
A dashboard focused on managing inventory levels, tracking stock movement, and improving supply chain efficiency.

**Key Highlights:**
- Stock level monitoring  
- Low stock alerts  
- Category-wise inventory analysis  
- Inventory movement trends
