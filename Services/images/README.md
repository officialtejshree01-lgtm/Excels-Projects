## 📊 Dashboard Overview  

![Customer Feedback Dashboard](dashboard.png)

The **Customer Feedback Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of customer satisfaction across different departments, products, and regions. It is designed to deliver quick insights through interactive visuals and KPI metrics.

---

## 🔍 Key Dashboard Insights  

- 📈 **Average Rating:** 3.02  
- 😊 **Positive Feedback:** 40%  
- ⚖️ **Balanced Sentiment Distribution:** Positive, Neutral, and Negative feedback are equally represented  

---

## 📊 Visual Analysis  

### 📦 Rating vs Product  
- Displays average ratings for each product category  
- Helps identify high-performing and low-performing products  

### 🧩 Sentiment Distribution  
- Pie chart showing proportion of Positive, Neutral, and Negative feedback  
- Provides quick understanding of overall customer sentiment  

### 🏢 Department vs Region vs Rating  
- Compares performance of departments across different regions  
- Identifies which department needs improvement in specific regions  

### 📉 Feedback Trend Across Products  
- Line chart showing trends of feedback (Positive, Neutral, Negative)  
- Helps track performance patterns across multiple products over time  

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Features  

- 🔘 **Department Filter:** App Usability, Billing, Delivery, Product Quality, Sales, Support  
- 🔘 **Product Filter:** Headphones, Jeans, Laptop, Mobile, etc.  
- 📌 Dynamic filtering updates all visuals instantly  

---

## 🎯 Business Value  

- Identifies areas with low customer satisfaction  
- Helps improve service quality and product performance  
- Supports data-driven decision making  
- Enables quick and effective reporting for management  

---

## 🚀 Summary  

This dashboard transforms raw customer feedback data into meaningful insights, making it easier to monitor performance, understand customer sentiment, and take actionable steps to enhance overall service quality.
